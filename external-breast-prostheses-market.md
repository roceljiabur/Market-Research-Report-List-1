<p><p>The external breast prostheses market is divided into two primary types: complete and partial prostheses. Complete prostheses replace the entire breast after mastectomy, providing a natural shape and weight to the chest for those who have undergone total breast removal. Partial prostheses, on the other hand, are designed for women who have had lumpectomies or partial mastectomies, offering support and enhancing symmetry for the remaining breast. Both types cater to different patient needs and preferences in breast reconstruction.</p></p>
