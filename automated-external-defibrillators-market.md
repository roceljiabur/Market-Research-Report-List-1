<p><p>The Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market comprises two main types: semi-automated and fully automated AEDs. Semi-automated devices guide users through voice prompts but require the responder to press a shock button when advised. In contrast, fully automated AEDs deliver shocks automatically without user intervention, enhancing ease of use, especially for untrained individuals. Both types are crucial in emergency medical situations, increasing survival rates for cardiac arrest patients while ensuring timely and effective treatment.</p></p>
