<p><p>The medical device pneumatic valves market comprises various types, including two-way, three-way, and four-way directional valves, along with others. Two-way directional valves control airflow in a single direction, ideal for simple on/off functions. Three-way valves allow for the diversion of air to two different outlets, facilitating more complex control. Four-way valves can manage multiple inputs and outputs, enabling intricate pneumatic systems. Other types may include specialized valves catering to specific medical applications, enhancing system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
