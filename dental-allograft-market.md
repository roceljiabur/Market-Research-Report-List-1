<p><strong>The Dental Allograft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Gingival Graft</li><li>Connective Tissue Graft</li><li>Pedicle Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental allograft market includes types such as free gingival graft, connective tissue graft, and pedicle graft, each serving specific periodontal needs. Free gingival grafts involve harvesting tissue from the palate to enhance keratinized gingiva. Connective tissue grafts are used to augment soft tissue in areas of recession, improving aesthetics and stability. Pedicle grafts utilize adjacent gingival tissue for grafting, minimizing donor site trauma. Together, these grafts address various dental restoration and periodontal health challenges.</p></p>
