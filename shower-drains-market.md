<p><strong>The Shower Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Drains</li><li>Linear Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shower drains market primarily consists of two types: point drains and linear drains. Point drains are traditional fixtures positioned at the center or corners of the shower floor, designed to collect water from surrounding areas. In contrast, linear drains provide a sleek, modern aesthetic, running along one edge of the shower. They effectively channel water along a longer surface, reducing the risk of pooling. Both types cater to different design preferences and functionality, influencing consumer choices in bathroom renovations.</p></p>
