<p><p>The klystron market is divided into two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) Klystrons and Pulse Klystrons. Continuous Wave Klystrons produce a constant output signal, ideal for applications in radar and communication systems requiring stable, high-frequency signals. In contrast, Pulse Klystrons generate short bursts of microwave power, making them suitable for pulsed radar applications and particle accelerators. Both types are essential in various sectors, including telecommunications, defense, and scientific research, driving advancements in microwave technology.</p></p>
