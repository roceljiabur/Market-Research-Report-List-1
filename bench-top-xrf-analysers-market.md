<p><strong>The Bench-top XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench-top XRF analyzers are essential tools for elemental analysis in various industries. They primarily come in two types: Wavelength Dispersive X-Ray Fluorescence (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive X-Ray Fluorescence (EDXRF). WDXRF offers high-resolution analysis by dispersing emitted X-rays based on their wavelength, making it ideal for trace elemental analysis. In contrast, EDXRF detects X-ray energies directly, providing rapid results and versatility for bulk materials. Both techniques enable effective material characterization and quality control across numerous applications.</p></p>
