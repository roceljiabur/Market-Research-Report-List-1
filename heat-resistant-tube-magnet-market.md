<p><p>The heat-resistant tube magnet market is segmented into single tube magnets and multiple tube magnets. Single tube magnets are designed for applications requiring focused magnetic separation in compact spaces, ideal for small-scale operations. In contrast, multiple tube magnets consist of several magnetic tubes arranged together, offering enhanced separation efficiency and broader coverage for larger operations. Both types are engineered to withstand high temperatures, making them suitable for industries like food processing and plastics where thermal stability is crucial.</p></p>
