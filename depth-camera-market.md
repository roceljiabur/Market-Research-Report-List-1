<p><p>The depth camera market encompasses various technologies designed to capture three-dimensional information. Structured-light cameras project a known pattern onto a scene and analyze distortion to gauge depth. Stereo vision cameras utilize two or more lenses to mimic human binocular vision, calculating depth through image disparity. Time-of-flight cameras measure the time it takes for emitted light to return after reflecting off objects, providing accurate distance measurements. Other market types may include LIDAR and infrared cameras, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
