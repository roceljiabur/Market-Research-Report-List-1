<p><strong>The Wafer Carrier Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer carrier boxes are specialized containers used in the semiconductor industry to transport and store wafers safely. The Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB) features a front-opening design for easy access, primarily used for shipping wafers in bulk, ensuring minimal exposure to contaminants. In contrast, the Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP) is designed for automated handling systems, providing superior protection and a standardized format. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining wafer integrity during transportation and storage processes.</p></p>
