<p><p>The single dose laundry detergent market is divided into two main types: non-bio laundry detergent and bio laundry detergent. Non-bio laundry detergents are formulated without enzymes, making them gentler on sensitive skin and preferred for delicate fabrics. They effectively clean stains but may require higher temperatures for optimal performance. In contrast, bio laundry detergents contain enzymes that break down organic stains, making them more effective at lower temperatures. This type appeals to environmentally conscious consumers seeking energy-efficient cleaning solutions.</p></p>
