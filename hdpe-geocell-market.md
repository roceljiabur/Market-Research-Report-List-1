<p><p>HDPE Geocell, or High-Density Polyethylene Geocell, is a three-dimensional cellular confinement system widely used in civil engineering and geotechnical applications. It consists of a series of interconnected cells that can be filled with soil, aggregate, or concrete to enhance stability and reduce erosion. The increasing demand for effective soil reinforcement and erosion control solutions is driving the market growth of HDPE Geocell. </p><p>The HDPE Geocell Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.8% during the forecast period.
