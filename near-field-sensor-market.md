<p><strong>The Near Field Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Sensors</li><li>Capacitive Sensors</li><li>Magnetic Sensors</li><li>Photoelectric & Ultrasonic Sensors</li><li>Optical Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Near Field Sensor market comprises several key types: </p><p>Inductive sensors detect metallic objects using electromagnetic fields. Capacitive sensors measure changes in capacitance to sense proximity or touch. Magnetic sensors detect magnetic fields, useful in position sensing. Photoelectric sensors utilize light beams to detect objects or changes in distance. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to measure distance and detect objects. Optical sensors use light to capture and interpret signals, offering high precision in various applications. Each type serves specific automation and detection needs across industries.</p></p>
