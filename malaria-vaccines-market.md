<p><strong>The Malaria Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Erythrocytic Vaccine</li><li>Erythrocytic Vaccine</li><li>Multi-antigen Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The malaria vaccine market is categorized into three main types: pre-erythrocytic vaccines, erythrocytic vaccines, and multi-antigen vaccines. Pre-erythrocytic vaccines target the liver stage of the malaria parasite, aiming to prevent infection before it enters the bloodstream. Erythrocytic vaccines focus on the blood stage, aiming to limit symptoms and reduce transmission during the disease's active phase. Multi-antigen vaccines combine multiple targets to elicit a broader immune response, providing enhanced protection against various malaria strains and stages.</p></p>
