<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis service market comprises two primary types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis. Hemodialysis involves filtering waste from the blood using an artificial kidney machine, typically performed in clinical settings. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the patient’s abdominal lining to filter blood, allowing for home-based treatment. Both modalities cater to individuals with kidney failure, offering flexibility in treatment options. The choice between them depends on factors like medical condition, lifestyle, and personal preference, influencing market dynamics and growth opportunities.</p></p>
