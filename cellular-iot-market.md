<p><strong>The Cellular IoT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2G</li><li>3G</li><li>4G</li><li>LTE–M</li><li>NB–LTE–M</li><li>NB–IoT</li><li>5G</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellular IoT market types refer to various technologies used for machine-to-machine (M2M) communications. 2G and 3G networks provide basic connectivity, but are largely phased out due to inefficiency. 4G LTE enables faster data rates. LTE-M and NB-LTE-M are optimized for low-power, wide-area applications. NB-IoT focuses on enhanced coverage and connectivity for numerous devices. Finally, 5G introduces ultra-reliable, low-latency communication, supporting advanced IoT applications with high data rates and massive device connectivity. Each type caters to different IoT requirements.</p></p>
