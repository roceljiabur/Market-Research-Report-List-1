<p><p>The cough syrup market is classified into three main types: cough suppressants, expectorants, and antihistamines. Cough suppressants inhibit the cough reflex, providing relief from dry coughs. Expectorants help loosen mucus, making it easier to expel, thus aiding in productive coughs. Antihistamines relieve allergy-related coughing by reducing histamine effects, which can cause respiratory symptoms. Each type serves specific needs, catering to different causes of cough and contributing to a diverse market aimed at enhancing respiratory health.</p></p>
