<p><p>The PTFE anti-wear agent market encompasses two primary types: monomer polymerization and resin degradation. Monomer polymerization involves the synthesis of PTFE from its monomers, resulting in high-performance materials with enhanced wear resistance. In contrast, resin degradation focuses on the breakdown of existing PTFE resins to create anti-wear agents, often improving their lubricating properties. Both methods cater to industries seeking reliable solutions for reducing friction and wear in various applications, enhancing equipment longevity and efficiency.</p></p>
