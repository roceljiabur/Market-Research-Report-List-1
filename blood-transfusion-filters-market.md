Whole blood transfusion filters are used to ensure the purity and safety of donated blood, removing harmful particles. Platelet transfusion filters focus on collecting and preserving platelets for patients with low platelet counts. Red cell transfusion filters specifically address the need for high-quality red blood cells, enhancing oxygen delivery in patients with anemia or during surgical procedures.</p></p>
