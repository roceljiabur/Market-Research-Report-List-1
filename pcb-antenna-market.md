<p><p>The PCB antenna market comprises various types, including Inverted-F Antennas (IFA), Monopole Inverted-F Antennas (MIFA), and other designs. IFA antennas are compact and provide good radiation efficiency, making them suitable for mobile devices. MIFA antennas enhance the performance of conventional IFAs, offering wider bandwidths and improved gain, ideal for multi-band applications. Other types encompass diverse configurations tailored for specific applications, including automotive, IoT, and Wi-Fi devices, each designed to meet unique operational requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
