<p><p>The portable refrigerant leak detectors market features two primary types: infrared and semiconductor devices. Infrared detectors utilize infrared sensors to identify refrigerant gases based on their unique absorption patterns, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Semiconductor detectors rely on chemical sensors that change resistance in response to gas presence, making them typically more cost-effective but potentially less precise. Both types are essential for maintaining equipment efficiency and safety in various applications, including HVAC systems and refrigeration units.</p></p>
