<p><p>Data loggers for cold chain monitoring are essential for ensuring temperature-sensitive products are stored and transported under optimal conditions. Offline data loggers operate independently, recording temperatures until retrieved for analysis. Cellular connected data loggers transmit real-time data via cellular networks, offering immediate alerts for temperature deviations. Wireless connected data loggers utilize Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to share data continuously with centralized systems, enabling efficient monitoring and quick response to maintain compliance and product integrity throughout the cold chain.</p></p>
