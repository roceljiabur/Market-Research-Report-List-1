<p><p>The submarine market can be classified into several types based on their capabilities and operational roles. Nuclear-Powered Ballistic Missile Submarines (SSBNs) serve as a strategic deterrent, carrying nuclear missiles for long-range strikes. Kilo-Class Submarines (SSKs), typically diesel-electric, are smaller, quieter, and primarily used for coastal defense and anti-submarine warfare, making them suitable for operations in shallower waters and near shorelines.</p></p>
