<p><p>The electric powertrain solution for the two-wheeler market encompasses two primary types: central motors and hub motors. Central motors are located near the vehicle's center of gravity, offering balanced weight distribution and better handling, ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, hub motors are integrated into the wheel itself, providing a compact design and reducing mechanical complexity. This configuration allows for easier installation and maintenance, appealing to urban riders. Both types cater to different consumer needs, enhancing the growing electrification of two-wheelers.</p></p>
