<p><p>The cellulose plates market encompasses types used in Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) and High-Performance Thin Layer Chromatography (HPTLC). TLC plates offer a simple, cost-effective method for separating compounds, suitable for various applications. In contrast, HPTLC plates provide enhanced resolution, speed, and automation for complex samples, making them ideal for rigorous analytical tasks. Both types rely on cellulose as a stationary phase, appealing to industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and environmental testing for their effectiveness in compound analysis and quality control.</p></p>
