<p><p>The aluminum solenoid valves market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Direct-acting Type**: These valves operate directly from the solenoid’s magnetic force, making them suitable for low flow applications.</p><p>2. **Sub-step Direct-acting Type**: This variant allows for finer control of flow rates through incremental opening steps, enhancing precision in applications.</p><p>3. **Pilot-type**: Utilizing a small pilot valve to control a larger valve, these are ideal for high flow applications, offering efficient operation and reduced energy consumption.</p></p>
