<p><p>The kitchen ventilator market is categorized into Side Suction, Near Suction, and Others. Side Suction ventilators are installed adjacent to the cooking surface, efficiently capturing smoke and odors from the side. Near Suction systems are positioned above the stove for direct extraction, maximizing air quality. Other types include downdraft and island hoods, catering to unique kitchen designs and preferences. Each type aims to enhance kitchen ventilation, ensuring a cleaner and healthier cooking environment while reducing smoke and grease accumulation.</p></p>
