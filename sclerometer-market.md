<p><strong>The Sclerometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brinell Sclerometer</li><li>Vickers Sclerometer</li><li>Rockwell Sclerometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sclerometer market comprises various types, including Brinell, Vickers, and Rockwell sclerometers. Brinell sclerometers measure hardness by pressing a hardened ball into the material and assessing the indentation size. Vickers sclerometers use a diamond indenter to evaluate hardness based on a specific load, allowing for versatile testing across different materials. Rockwell sclerometers provide quick hardness readings using a minor load followed by a major load. Other types may include Knoop and Shore sclerometers, which serve specialized applications in hardness testing.</p></p>
