<p><p>The industrial-grade HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose) market is segmented into low, middle, and high viscosity types, each catering to different applications. Low viscosity HPMC is typically used for processes requiring rapid dissolution and fluidity, such as in coatings and adhesives. Middle viscosity HPMC offers a balance between solubility and thickening, making it suitable for construction applications like mortars and plasters. High viscosity HPMC is ideal for products that need significant thickening and gel-forming properties, commonly found in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
