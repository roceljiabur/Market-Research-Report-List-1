<p><p>The aluminum sheet market comprises two primary types: cold-rolled and hot-rolled aluminum sheets. Cold-rolled aluminum sheets are produced at room temperature, resulting in a smooth surface and higher dimensional accuracy, making them ideal for applications requiring precision, such as in automotive and aerospace industries. In contrast, hot-rolled aluminum sheets are processed at elevated temperatures, allowing for better malleability and thickness but with a rougher surface finish. These sheets are commonly used in construction and heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
