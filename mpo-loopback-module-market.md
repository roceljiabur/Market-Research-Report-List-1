<p><p>The MPO Loopback Module market is categorized into two main types: Single Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber loopback modules. Single Mode Fiber loopback modules are designed for long-distance data transmission and typically use a single light path, making them suitable for high-bandwidth applications. In contrast, Multimode Fiber loopback modules utilize multiple light paths, enabling efficient data transfer over shorter distances, ideal for local area networks and data centers. Both types are essential for testing and maintaining network integrity.</p></p>
