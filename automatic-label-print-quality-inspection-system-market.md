<p><strong>The Automatic Label Print Quality Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Inline"</li><li>"Offline"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Label Print Quality Inspection System market is divided into two main types: inline and offline. Inline systems are integrated directly into the production line, allowing real-time inspection of labels as they are printed, ensuring immediate feedback and corrections. Offline systems, on the other hand, operate separately from the production line, where printed labels are inspected after the printing process. This allows for batch evaluations and can be useful for more detailed analysis of label quality without disrupting production flow.</p></p>
