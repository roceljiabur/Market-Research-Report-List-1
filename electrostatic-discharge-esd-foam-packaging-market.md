<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Foam Packaging is categorized into three types: conductive, dissipative, and metal. Conductive foam is made from carbon-filled materials, providing a path for static charges to dissipate, protecting sensitive electronics. Dissipative foam, while not as conductive as conductive foam, reduces charge buildup, offering moderate protection for components. Metal foam incorporates metallic elements, creating an efficient barrier against ESD while providing structural support. Each type serves specific applications based on the level of protection required for electronic components.</p></p>
