<p><p>The Active Sonar System market comprises various types, notably Multi-Beam and Side Scan Sonar systems. Multi-Beam Sonar systems emit multiple sonar beams, providing detailed bathymetric maps and enhancing underwater surveys by covering vast areas efficiently. In contrast, Side Scan Sonar systems are designed to produce imagery of the seafloor by surveying wide swathes laterally, perfect for detecting submerged objects and mapping underwater habitats. Both technologies are crucial for marine exploration, archaeological investigations, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
